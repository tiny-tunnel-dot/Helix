# Peptide Protocol Calendar

**8-week cycle: Friday May 1, 2026 → Friday June 26, 2026**

## Reconstitution

Add **2mL bacteriostatic water** to each 20mg combo vial.

- **Vial contents:** 10mg of each peptide (20mg total per combo vial)
- **Concentration after mixing:** 5mg per mL of each peptide
- **Method:** Inject water slowly down the inside wall, gentle swirl until dissolved (no shaking)
- **Storage:** Refrigerate after mixing, label with date

| Post-workout / midday | BPC/TB | 0.1mL | **10 units** | Shoulder or stomach |
| Before bed (2-3hr after food) | CJC/Ipa | 0.05mL | **5 units** | Stomach |

**Per-injection peptide dose:**
- CJC-1295: 250mcg per injection (500mcg/day total)
- Ipamorelin: 250mcg per injection (500mcg/day total)
- BPC-157: 500mcg/day
- TB-500: 500mcg/day

**Schedule:** CJC/Ipa runs on a rolling **5-on / 2-off** cycle starting Fri May 1 (first off-days are Wed–Thu May 6–7, then May 13–14, etc.). BPC/TB runs every day. (5-on/2-off prevents GH receptor desensitization.)

## Monitoring During Cycle (with GLP-1)

- **Glucose:** Check fasting glucose periodically. The CJC/Ipa can raise it slightly while the GLP-1 lowers it. Watch for trends outside your normal range.
- **Hydration & electrolytes:** GLP-1 reduces intake; CJC/Ipa shifts water. Stay ahead of it.
- **Protein intake:** Target 1g per pound of bodyweight even with reduced appetite from GLP-1.
- **Side effects to flag:** Hand tingling/numbness, joint swelling, persistent nausea, unusual fatigue.

## After June 26

Reassess shoulder pain, sleep quality, body composition. Options:
- Stop cycle, take 2-4 weeks off, see how shoulder holds
- Run a second BPC/TB cycle if shoulder needs more work
- Take a full month off CJC/Ipa before considering another GH cycle
